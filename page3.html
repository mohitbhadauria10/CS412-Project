Confirmed and Death Cases Trend in United States

This below chart shows the trend of COVID-19 confirmed cases and also death cases in United States from Jan 2020 to July 2021. Confirmed cases trend are shown in blue while number of death cases trend versus reporting date is shown in red color.
User can scroll over any trend (confirmed or death) and see cumulative number of confirmed or death cases in a selected date.
This chart also specify the major dates when infections were peaked in terms of COVID first wave and second wave and also when infections started declining because of vaccine roll out.
